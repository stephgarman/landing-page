Cat Identification: Common Colors & Patterns

The tabby cat - often a brown cat with black stripes - is one of the most common combinations of color and pattern.
Calico cats are tri-color (black, orange & white) and are almost always female.
Tortoiseshell, or simply "tortie" cats, are orange & black. Torties are also female and are famous for their "tortietude" or sassy personalites.
White cats are less common and are more likely to be deaf.
