Types of Coat Colors & Patterns

The brown tabby is a brown cat - a brown cat with black stripes - is one of the most common combinations of color and pattern.
Calico cats are tri-color and are almost always female.
Like the calico cat, torties are almost always female and are famous for their "tortietude" or sassy personality.
White cats are less common and are more likely to be deaf.
